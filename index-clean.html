Instructions

In this study, you will paired with a live partner (from Mechanical Turk or from Facebook) and will compete against another team of Turkers and/or Facebook users in a game involving American history.

Each team will have a questioner and an answerer. The questioner will select questions from a large pool, and the answerer will answer each question. Each team will tackle 10 questions, and the team with the greater number of correct answers could earn up to 5 dollars, depending on the margin of difference between the two teams.
