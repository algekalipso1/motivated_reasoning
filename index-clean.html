Instructions

In this study, you will paired with a live partner from Mechanical Turk and will compete against another team of Turkers and in a word game involving rearranging letters to form words.

Each team will have a word selector and a word unscrambler. The word selector will select a word from a list of 6-letter words and enter it into a word scrambler, then the word unscrambler will have 20 seconds to figure out the original word. Each team will repeat this process for ten words, and the team with the greater number of correct answers could earn up to 1 dollar, depending on the margin of difference between the two teams.
