Instructions

In this study, you will paired with a live partner (from Mechanical Turk or from Facebook) and will compete against another team of Turkers and/or Facebook users in a word game involving anagrams.

Each team will have a questioner and an answerer. The questioner will select a 6-letter word and enter it into a word scrambler, then the answerer will have 20 seconds to figure out the original word. Each team will repeat this process for ten words, and the team with the greater number of correct answers could earn up to 5 dollars, depending on the margin of difference between the two teams.
